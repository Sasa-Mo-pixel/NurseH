💉 DKA

Definition

Diabetic Ketoacidosis is a serious complication of diabetes characterized by hyperglycemia, ketosis, and metabolic acidosis.

Causes

  • Infection
  • Missed insulin
  • Stress

Symptoms

  • Polyuria
  • Kussmaul breathing
  • Fruity breath

Management

  • IV fluids first
  • Insulin therapy
  • Monitor potassium
