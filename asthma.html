🫁 Asthma

Definition

Asthma is a chronic inflammatory disease of the airways leading to reversible airway obstruction.

Causes

  • Allergens
  • Exercise
  • Cold air

Symptoms

  • Wheezing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Cough

Management

  • Oxygen
  • Bronchodilators
  • Steroids
